# Governance, Risk and Compliance (GRC)

Notes and practical exercises covering cybersecurity governance, risk management, compliance frameworks, and security assurance.

## Topics
- Governance and Risk Management
- ISO/IEC 27001
- NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF)
- Risk Assessments and Risk Registers
- Security Policies and Standards
- Third-Party Risk Management (TPRM)
- Internal Audits and Gap Assessments
- Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery

> Effective cybersecurity is not only about technology. It is also about governance, accountability, and managing risk.
